# 🕷️ The Price Tracker: Automated Competitor Intelligence

## 📊 The Business Problem
E-commerce businesses lose margins daily because they cannot manually monitor competitor pricing changes fast enough. Checking hundreds of product pages by hand is a waste of payroll.

## 💡 The Solution
A lightweight, stealthy Python web scraper (`BeautifulSoup` + `requests`) that automatically navigates competitor websites, extracts live pricing data, and formats it into structured intelligence reports.

## 📈 Business Impact & ROI
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Allows businesses to automatically adjust their own prices based on market movements.
* **Alert System:** Flags undervalued or heavily discounted products instantly, enabling fast strategic decisions.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
* **Stack:** Python, `requests`, `BeautifulSoup4`, `Pandas`
* **Features:** DOM parsing, data cleaning, automated CSV report generation with conditional alert tagging.
